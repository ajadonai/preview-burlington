Services

Every engagement is built around your specific record

Burlington Consult does not apply templates. The most consequential decision in any petition — the field of endeavour — is developed from your professional record, stress-tested, and mapped to USCIS evidentiary standards before any drafting begins.

EB-1A & EB-2 NIW Dual Petition

Filed simultaneously as standard practice. This preserves the same priority date across both pathways, creates strategic redundancy, and allows a single unified evidence record to be built once and deployed across both petitions.

Proposed Endeavour Framework

Burlington identifies and develops the strongest available field of endeavour, tests it against all three Dhanasar prongs, and confirms alignment with the documented evidence base before any drafting begins.

Full EB-1A Petition (I-140)

Criterion-by-criterion argument construction covering all applicable extraordinary ability criteria, with evidence curation, contextualisation, and cross-referencing to USCIS standards.

Full EB-2 NIW Petition (I-140)

Dhanasar three-prong framework: substantial merit and national importance, well-positioned argument, and the national interest waiver balancing test — each constructed from your specific record.

Letters of Support (up to 12)

Each letter researched and drafted from scratch. Burlington identifies the strongest recommender composition, drafts each letter to address specific USCIS criteria, and coordinates sign-off.

Evidence Architecture

Full documentary record curated, formatted, and presented to USCIS standards. Independent corroborating evidence sourced. Every exhibit cross-referenced to a specific criterion.

12-Month Retainer

From the date of submission: advisory support covering any RFE or NOID, all follow-up documentation requests, and written guidance on every USCIS communication. At no additional charge.
